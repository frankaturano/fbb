IPA
Our IPA is a modern take on the West Coast original. We add the majority of the hops late in the brewing process resulting in a huge hop aroma of citrus fruits balanced with herbs and pine. Flaked wheat and Optic malt add some complexity and body, but this beer is all about hop aroma and bitterness.

Hoppy Lager
At a higher ABV, our Hoppy Lager is a departure from the more traditional lagers we brew. We use a light grain bill and our clean-fermenting house lager yeast to create the perfect base to showcase large additions of Motueka and Centennial hops. This gives the beer an aroma of tropical fruit, lime zest, grapefruit, and light pine.
